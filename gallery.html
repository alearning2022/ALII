Vision

Academic Learning Institution will be the second home for young shildren where academic readiness and excellence are attained in nurturing, fun and supportive learning environment.

Mission

To meet the developmental needs of the whole child by providing experiences that are challenging and promote social emotional, physical and academic growth, through an experience, guided student activities and student involvement in classroom management. The children will be provided the opportunity to develop a positive self-concept and a strong sense of belongingness which will enable each child to reach his/her ptotential for success in school life.

Humble Beginning

The Academic Learning Institution was established by Mrs. Cleofe S. Casquejo in 2008.

The idea was conceptualized as a result of her passion for education, having been an educator for 43 years. Her dream to establush a school was hastened when the need of kindergarten teachers and parents who are worried because of the closure of the kindergarten Program of one of the public elementary school in the area.

Upon retirements, she immediately looked for an ample space to be rented to serve the purpose of the school the concern on qualified faculty member who will teach the kindergarten students was solved by the willingness of the qualified kindergarten teachers who are already unemployed because of the temporary closure of the said kindergarten program.

The school was originally located right infront of Matina Central Elementary School and later transferred to Topaz Street. Sto Nino Matina Davao City, and in 2015 it transferred to its permanent location at Sunrise Village, Matina Davao City.
